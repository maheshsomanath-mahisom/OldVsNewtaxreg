Old Tax Regime vs New Tax Regime

The Income Tax Department provides taxpayers with two options: Old Tax Regime and New Tax Regime. The old regime allows various deductions such as Section 80C, 80D, HRA exemption and home loan benefits. The new regime provides lower tax rates with limited deductions.

Old Tax Regime

Under the old tax regime, taxpayers can reduce taxable income by claiming eligible deductions including investments under Section 80C, medical insurance under Section 80D, NPS contribution and housing loan interest.

New Tax Regime

The new tax regime provides revised tax slabs with fewer exemptions. It is suitable for taxpayers who do not claim many deductions.

Which Tax Regime is Better?

The better tax regime depends on your income, investments, deductions and exemptions. Use this calculator to compare both options and select the regime with lower tax liability.
